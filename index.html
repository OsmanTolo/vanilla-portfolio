A little About Me

Greetings! I'm Osman, a web developer who loves bringing ideas to life on the internet. My passion for web development was ignited during the pandemic lockdown in 2020, when I revisited coding after initially despising it during my degree program. Seeing my code come to life on a website was exhilarating!

Now, I am a graduate of a Front-end Web Development Boot Camp, and have extensive experience in building web applications using a variety of technologies. My current focus is on developing inclusive and accessible digital experiences with ReactJs, which is a powerful tool for creating interactive and dynamic user interfaces.
